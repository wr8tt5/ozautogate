How does it work?

The Autogate is unique in several ways. It requires no power source to operate. It will operate safely and efficiently with any size vehicle, and it can be installed in minutes with no special tools required.

The Autogate system relies on the weight of a vehicle on the ramps to open the gate. This ensures that no matter what size vehicle or number of axles the vehicle may have, the Autogate cannot begin to close until the ramps are cleared. The Autogate will also operate for a pedestrian who puts their weight on the ramp. An ingenious spring system ensures that even the youngest of visitors can gain access with the Autogate system installed.

The simplicity of installation is another area where Autogate excels. Two people can unpack and install the gate in minutes. Simply position the gate, fit the two operating ramps, engage the mechanisms, adjust the closure speed, and your Autogate is ready to go. If for any reason you wish to disable the gate's operation, simply raise the arm to release the ramp. Reconnecting is easily done by lifting the ramp to re-engage the arm. If the gate needs to be held open, operate it manually and engage the locking bar.

Autogate is strong and safe, and can be utilised by any vehicle. The installation is simplicity in itself. The instruction sheet is set out step by step and supplemented with clear illustrations.
